Simple Wrapper around `hugo new`

with tags hugo - Monday, August 17, 2015

I am slightly anal about how my files are structured on disk. To this end I like to date prefix my post files. Hugo does not easily accommodate this and I found that it was causing me to not write blog posts or quick notes when I otherwise would have.

To fix the issue I wrote a small little script: newpost.zsh. I can either call it as a one off, or from a make new to easily kick off new posts.

It does require ZSH as I am using some of the nifty parameter expansion that is unique to ZSH.

Here is an example of a make new run, and the resulting output:
